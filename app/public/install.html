Install it on your phone — it opens like a normal app, with no Play Store or App Store needed.

1

2

3

Then come back here — the Install button will work.

📲 One tap to install

Tap the button and confirm Install. GymSetu will be added to your home screen.

1

Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right).

2

Tap “Install app” or “Add to Home screen”.

📲 Add to your Home Screen

1

Tap the Share button ↑ at the bottom of Safari.

2

Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”.

3

Tap Add — GymSetu appears on your home screen like any app.
