01 — Growth Phases (Month by Month)
PHASE 1 — MONTH 1–2
Hustle & Validate
Target: 0 → 5 paying gyms · Focus: offline only

WHAT TO DO

  • Visit 10 gyms in your city. Walk in. Ask to speak to owner.
  • Give a 10-minute live demo on your phone
  • Offer first month FREE or at ₹499 to get foot in door
  • Ask for harsh feedback — what's missing, what's confusing
  • Fix everything they complain about immediately
  • Get 1 testimonial (video or WhatsApp screenshot) from first gym

WHAT NOT TO DO

  • Don't run paid ads yet — waste of money
  • Don't build new features — fix what exists
  • Don't spend on Instagram ads before 5 paying gyms
  • Don't skip feedback — these first 5 gyms shape everything
  • Don't over-promise features you haven't built
PHASE 2 — MONTH 3–4
Launch & Campaign
Target: 5 → 20 gyms · Focus: offline + first Instagram reels

WHAT TO DO

  • Post 4 reels/week — Problem-Solution + Demo + Behind Build
  • Start ₹5,000/month Instagram ads (Video Views objective)
  • Your team does offline gym visits in parallel with content
  • Send WhatsApp cold outreach to gym owners (200+ DMs/week)
  • Offer 7-day free trial — no card required
  • Reply to every comment and DM within 1 hour

METRICS TO TRACK

  • Reel views per post (target: 1K+ per reel)
  • DM conversations started per week
  • Demo calls booked vs showed up
  • Trial to paid conversion rate (target: 40%+)
  • Cost per lead from Instagram ads
PHASE 3 — MONTH 5–6
Scale & Systematize
Target: 20 → 42 gyms · Focus: referrals + Tier 2 launch

WHAT TO DO

  • Launch referral program — ₹300 credit per gym they refer
  • Launch Tier 2 features, upsell existing gyms
  • Build partnerships with fitness equipment dealers
  • Create a landing page with booking form for demos
  • Increase ads to ₹8K/month with Lead Gen objective
  • Start expanding to 1 or 2 nearby cities

WHAT TO BUILD

  • WhatsApp automation (reminder messages)
  • PDF receipt generation
  • Expense tracker + break-even dashboard
  • Onboarding flow for new gym owners
  • Basic landing page with demo booking
PHASE 4 — MONTH 7–9
Multi-City & Automation
Target: 42 → 70 gyms · Focus: new cities + online inbound

WHAT TO DO

  • Assign your offline team to dedicated cities
  • Create city-specific Instagram content ("Mumbai gym owners...")
  • Build a simple landing page (gymsetu.in) with video demo
  • Start collecting Google Reviews from happy gym owners
  • Partner with personal trainer communities and networks
  • Set up automated email drip for trial users who don't convert

KPIs AT THIS STAGE

  • Monthly churn rate must be under 5%
  • NPS (Net Promoter Score) — ask gyms monthly
  • Revenue per gym (push Tier 2 upsells)
  • Referrals per month — target 5+ from existing gyms
  • Cost per acquisition going down over time
PHASE 5 — MONTH 10–12
100 Gyms & Premium
Target: 70 → 100 gyms · Focus: brand authority + Phase 4 features

WHAT TO DO

  • Launch Phase 4 premium features (QR attendance, AI health score)
  • Create a "GymSetu Partner" program for top gym owners
  • Get featured in Indian fitness/startup media
  • Build case study content: "How [Gym Name] grew 30% with GymSetu"
  • Consider hiring 1 full-time sales person for offline expansion

REVENUE GOAL

  • 100 gyms @ avg ₹1,200/gym = ₹1,20,000 MRR

  • Begin planning for 500 gym milestone
02 — Offline Sales Strategy (Your #1 Weapon)
Why offline first? Indian gym owners trust people they've met in person. A 15-minute in-person demo converts at 40–60%. An Instagram ad converts at 1–3%. Your offline visits will generate 10x the ROI of any digital campaign in the first 3 months.

DEMO SALES SCRIPT — WORD FOR WORD

OPENING — When you walk in "Bhai, namaskar — main Nikhil hoon. Aapka hi gym hai? Main ek app banaya hai sirf gym owners ke liye. Kya main 5 minute mein ek cheez dikhata hoon? Achha lagega pakka."
PAIN OPENER — Create the itch "Aap abhi members ko kaise track karte ho? Register mein? WhatsApp pe? Excel?" [Wait for answer — they'll say register or WhatsApp] "Haan, yahi problem hai — jab 50-100 members ho jaate hain, manually sab track karna mushkil ho jaata hai. Dues pata nahi, expiry pata nahi, month end pe pata nahi kitna aaya. Sahi hai na?"
DEMO — Show, don't tell "Dekhiye — yeh GymSetu hai. [Open app — show dashboard] Yeh hai aapka poora gym ek screen pe. Yeh dekho — 6 memberships is hafte expire ho rahi hain. Automatic alert aa jaata hai. Yeh dekho — dues section — har member ka pending amount. Yeh dekho — WhatsApp reminder — member ko khud jaata hai bina aap kuch kiye."
CLOSE — The trial offer "7 din bilkul free trial hai — koi card nahi chahiye. Abhi install karte hain — main live setup kar deta hoon, 10 minute lagenge. Aapke pehle 5 members main khud daal deta hoon system mein. Phir aap khud dekh lena."
OBJECTION: "Baad mein dekhenge" "Bilkul bhai — meri ek request hai — sirf pehle 3 members daal lete hain abhi, 2 minute mein. Phir aap khud decide karo. App delete bhi kar sakte ho — koi binding nahi." Tip: If they let you install → 70% convert to paid. The "later" objection disappears once they see their own data in the app.

06 — Retention Strategy (Keep Gyms from Leaving)
The leaky bucket problem: If you add 10 gyms/month but lose 4, you only net 6. Churn is the silent killer of SaaS businesses. Your goal: keep monthly churn under 5% (less than 1 gym lost per 20 gyms). A gym that stays for 12 months gives you ₹11,988–₹19,188 in lifetime value.
Retention ActionWhenMethodGoal
Onboarding Call Day 1 of trial Personal WhatsApp call — 20 min Get them to add 10+ members before Day 3. Activated users almost never churn.
Day 3 Check-in Day 3 of trial WhatsApp message "Kuch issue aaya? Ek feature batao jo miss ho raha hai." Fix it within 48 hours if possible.
Week 1 Feature Tip Day 7 WhatsApp + in-app notification Show one "wow" feature they may have missed — e.g., WhatsApp reminder or revenue chart.
Monthly Success Check Day 30 (monthly) WhatsApp message "Bhai, is month kitna collect hua GymSetu se? Kuch naya chahiye kya?" This keeps them engaged.
Renewal Reminder 5 days before renewal WhatsApp + in-app banner Never let a gym churn by accident because they forgot. Remind proactively.
Win-back Campaign 3 days after churn Personal WhatsApp call "Kya hua bhai? Koi problem thi? Batao — main fix kar deta hoon." 30% of churned users come back if you call within a week.
Feature Announcement When new feature launches WhatsApp broadcast + reel Keeps gyms excited. Every new feature they use = harder to leave.
TARGET CHURN RATE: Under 5%/month (no more than 1 gym lost per 20 active). Average gym lifetime: 12+ months.

Partnerships = free customers. If someone who gym owners already trust recommends GymSetu, you skip the entire trust-building process. These partners become your sales force without being on your payroll.
