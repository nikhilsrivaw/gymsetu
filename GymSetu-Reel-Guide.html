00 — The Golden Rule of Reels
You have 1.7 seconds. That's how long someone takes to scroll past your reel if the first frame doesn't grab them. The hook is everything. Your first 3 seconds must either create CURIOSITY ("how is that possible?"), show PAIN ("does your gym owner still use notebooks?"), or trigger DESIRE ("this gym earns ₹1.2L/month, here's how"). Everything else — the app demo, the features, the CTA — only matters if they stay for second 4.
The formula that works: Hook (0–3s) → Problem/Pain (3–8s) → Solution reveal (8–18s) → Quick demo (18–35s) → Strong CTA (35–45s). Total: 45–60 seconds is the sweet spot. Under 30s feels rushed. Over 90s loses people.

08 — Do's & Don'ts
❌
Don't start with "Hi guys, today I'm going to show you..."You've already lost them. Jump straight into the hook with energy.
❌
Don't use bad lighting or shaky cameraFilm near a window in daylight. Use your phone stabilizer or just prop it against something. Bad quality = low trust.
❌
Don't post without captions / subtitles70% of Instagram is watched without sound. If you don't have text on screen, they don't understand your reel. Add subtitles in CapCut always.
❌
Don't use 30+ hashtagsOld strategy, actually hurts reach in 2025. Use 5–10 super relevant ones only.
❌
Don't boost a reel with under 300 organic viewsMeta's AI will see that real people didn't engage and will show it to low-quality audiences. Only boost winners.
✅
Reply to EVERY comment within the first 2 hoursInstagram's algorithm rewards engagement velocity. Reply fast = more distribution = more views. Even just a "thanks!" counts.
✅
Ask a question in your caption to drive comments"Teri gym mein kitne members hain? Comment mein bata" — comments boost reach dramatically. The algorithm loves it.
✅
Use trending audio in the background (even at low volume)Trending audio gets algorithmic boost. Pick a song from Instagram's Trending section. Keep at 10–15% volume so voice stays clear.
✅
Save your best scripts and hooks — repeat them with variationsIf a hook worked once, test the same hook with different visuals. Don't reinvent the wheel every time.
✅
DM everyone who comments or saves your reel"Hey bhai, dekha tune reel — tera gym hai kya? Demo dikhana chahiye?" — 40% conversion rate from comment to DM conversation.
